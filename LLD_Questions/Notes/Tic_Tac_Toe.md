# Tic Tac Toe:

## Rules of the Game:

- Its a simple two player game
- Each player select a piece before starting a game
- Players take turn alternately and place the piece on a 3x3 matrix
- A player wins the game by placing 3 of its pieces in a column, row or diagonal
- A game ends when a player wins or when all the boxes are filled which results in a draw.

## Objects invovled

- Playing Piece
- Board: NxN
- Player
